About Us

With AidX, donors can trace their contributions in real-time, ensuring that their aid reaches its intended recipients without undue administrative costs or intermediaries. Likewise, charitable organizations can leverage AidX to access a global pool of donors, verify their credibility, and receive instant, borderless transactions. AidX is not just a cryptocurrency; it's a movement dedicated to making charitable giving more impactful and restoring trust in the process.

Tokenomics

Staking

Holders can stake their AidX tokens, earning interest while simultaneously directing a portion of their staking rewards to charities of their choice..

Transparency Ledger

Each AidX donation transaction will be recorded transparently on the blockchain, allowing donors to verify the end-use of their contributions.

Burn Mechanism

0.5% of each transaction will be burned, ensuring the long-term value and sustainability of the AidX ecosystem.

Partnerships

Strategic partnerships with NGOs, charitable foundations, and social enterprises will help drive adoption and ensure the token's primary use-case remains philanthropic.

Security

Regular audits will be conducted to ensure the safety and security of the AidX network.
